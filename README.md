> Named after **Elis Regina** (1945-1982), Brazil's greatest singer, known as "The Voice of Brazil". Just as Elis conducted stages with intensity and perfection, this orchestrator conducts AI agents to create world-class digital work.

## The Elis Philosophy

Elis Regina wasn't just a singer — she was a **conductor** of emotions. Every performance was a perfect orchestration of voice, band, arrangement, and soul. She demanded excellence from herself and everyone around her.

**Elis** (the orchestrator) carries that spirit:

- **Uncompromising excellence** — Every output must be worthy of awwwards.com
- **Perfect orchestration** — Each agent enters at the right moment, with the right context
- **Soul in the work** — It's not just code, it's digital art
- **Intensity** — Full delivery, from discovery to deploy

## What is Elis?

**Elis** is a skill for [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code) that transforms Claude into a maestro of specialized agents. Just as Elis Regina conducted her performances with precision and passion, this orchestrator coordinates 10+ agents to create world-class one-page websites.
